About Maxwell

Maxwell Oti is a senior executive with over 15 years of leadership across energy, manufacturing, technology, and financial services. He has held senior roles with Shell and Schlumberger and currently serves as Vice President, Operations, Systems & Strategy at Diverge Manufacturing. His work spans governance, strategic oversight, operational excellence, and risk management.

He has overseen budgets exceeding $100M, led multi‑stakeholder initiatives across Canada and abroad, and built high‑performing teams that drive innovation and sustainable results. His experience covers supply chain optimization, enterprise risk, audit compliance, and financial stewardship — all essential for effective board governance.

Beyond the corporate world, Maxwell is committed to community impact through youth digital literacy, small business capacity‑building, and mentorship. His ability to bridge corporate leadership with community advancement aligns closely with cooperative values and member‑driven organizations.
